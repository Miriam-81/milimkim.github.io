About Me

Hi, I am Milim Kim, a professional and certified Korean language tutor based in the UK. With over 10 years of extensive experience in second language education, I am dedicated to helping students of all levels master the Korean language.

Before relocating to the UK in 2012, I spent 8 years working as a licensed English teacher in public middle schools in South Korea after passing the rigorous national employment exam. This background provided me with a deep, systematic understanding of language acquisition and classroom management. Since May 2017, I have successfully taught over 50 students from diverse cultural backgrounds, ranging from secondary school students to full-time professionals, offering both tailored 1-to-1 lessons and engaging group sessions.

My teaching methodology focuses on practical communication, interactive multi-media resources, and customized curricula designed around each student's unique goals—whether it is conversational fluency, business proficiency, or TOPIK preparation. I hold a Certificate of Korean Language Teacher (Level 2) issued by the Korean Government, ensuring the highest standards of professional instruction.

I also run a YouTube channel, Milim Kim Korean, where I share valuable language learning materials and insights to help independent learners.
